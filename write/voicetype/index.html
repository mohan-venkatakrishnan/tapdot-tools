tapdot / VoiceType

VoiceType

Dictate text using your browser's built-in speech recognition.

Unlike the rest of tapdot, this tool is not fully local. Browser speech recognition (Chrome's SpeechRecognition API) sends your microphone audio to your browser vendor's speech-to-text servers (e.g. Google) to produce the transcript — tapdot itself never sees or stores it, but it does leave your device. See Privacy for details. Don't dictate anything sensitive.

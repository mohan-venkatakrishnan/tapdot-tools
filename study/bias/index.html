Turn on your browser's built-in AI

BiasCheck's full analysis runs on Chrome's on-device model (Gemini Nano). Enable these two flags, then relaunch Chrome. Use Chrome 128+ on desktop with ~22 GB free.

chrome://flags/#prompt-api-for-gemini-nano → Enabled
chrome://flags/#optimization-guide-on-device-model → Enabled BypassPerfRequirement
  1. Paste each flag address into your address bar (browsers block links to chrome://, so copy & paste).
  2. Set them as shown above, then click Relaunch.
  3. Come back and press Analyse — the model downloads once, then runs offline.
